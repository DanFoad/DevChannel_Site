About DevChannel

What is the DevChannel?

We are a community of differently skilled programmers (from begginers to experts) who hangout in a Slack chatroom. The goal is to help and be helped, share ideas and make innovative projects together, all of this in a friendly environment.

How we started...

About two months ago, therightman_ posted a thread on /r/learnprogramming about how he wanted to have some friends to learn programming with, which got a good amount of replies. He then started a slack team with about 40 people who wanted to join and their journey started...
