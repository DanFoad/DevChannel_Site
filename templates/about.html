About DevChannel

What is DevChannel?

The main goals of the community are to:

  • Show the path to beginner programmers who need or want help;
  • Make projects with others, from the simplest calculator to the most innovative creations;
  • Share ideas;
  • Help each other and learn together!

We are all part of a Slack team (Slack is a platform for team communication) and talk there with each other.

Some of the things you'll find in DevChannel:

  • Channels about different programming topics!
  • Other people to make projects with!
  • Programmers (beginner to advanced) that share knowledge with each other!

How we started...

Have you ever wanted to program with friends? Well, I have, and as a result I posted a thread on reddit a while back. I expected like 1 or 2 replies from some other lonely beginners, but instead I got a lot more and someone suggested that I create a Slack team, which is what I did. From a community of about 40 people we started DevChannel, which now has almost 400 members!
